Ramen

Description

Favored by many, and especially by Japanese culture fans soup-dish with noodles, vegetables and eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 ginger (cut into 4 slices)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (peeled and smashed)
  • 1 tablespoon of miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon of sambal oelek
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Rice wine vinegar(dash)
  • 2 blocks of ramen
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • scallions(thinly sliced)
  • black sesame seeds(or togarashi
  • 1/2 piece of nori(thinly sliced!)

Steps

  1. Cook your eggs now, or make them ahead of time and refrigerate until ready to use.
  2. Combine the broth, ginger, garlic, miso, sambal oelek, soy sauce, sesame oil, and vinegar in a saucepan. Bring everything to a boil and reduce the heat to let it simmer. Keep at a simmer for 10 minutes, then spoon out the ginger and the garlic slices.
  3. Add the dried noodles and cook until they are soft. Use tongs to divide the noodles evenly between two bowls. Cover with broth and top with halved eggs, scallions, black sesame seeds or togarashi, and nori.
