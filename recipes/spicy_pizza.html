Spicy Pizza

Description

Let me share with you an easy recipe for hot & spicy pizza I've found in the internet.

Ingredients

  • 1 quantity pizza dough or pizza mix made according to manufacturers instructions
  • 1 can of tomato puree or spicy pizza sauce
  • Any meat of your choice
  • 4 ounces of Mozarella Cheese
  • 1/2 red onion(sliced in rings)
  • 2 ounces of pickled Jalapenos
  • 3 teaspoons of dried oregano

Steps

  1. Roll out dough into 2 or 4 equal portions, spread with tomato puree or pizza sauce.
  2. Add meat (if using) and mozzarella cheese, sprinkle with oregano and top with red onion and jalapenos.
  3. Bake at 230 degrees celcius for around 10-13 minutes, or until cheese has melted. Remove pizza from the oven and let it cool off for about 10 minutes before slicing.
