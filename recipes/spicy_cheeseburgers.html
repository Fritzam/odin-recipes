Spicy Cheeseburgers

Description

I'm not a cook, but this recipe has 4/5 stars with 2 reviews so I think we'll be fine...propably

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of Worcestshire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of Liquid Smoke(optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • 3 pounds of ground beef
  • 9 slices of pepper Jack cheese
  • 9 hamburger buns(split)
  • Lettuce leaves and tomato slices(optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the first 10 ingredients. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well. Shape into nine patties.
  2. Grill, uncovered, over medium heat for 7-8 minutes on each side or until no longer pink. Top with cheese. Grill 3-5 minutes for longer or until cheese is melted. Serve on buns with lettuce and tomato if desired.
